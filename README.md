Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2-11-2026

Overview

Carlink (“the App”) is designed to enable Apple CarPlay functionality through a compatible USB adapter on supported Android Automotive systems.

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data with external servers.

All processing occurs locally on the device.

Information Collection

The App does not collect or transmit personal information to any external server.

The App may request access to:

Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO) — Used to support voice features such as Siri and hands-free calling when connected to Apple CarPlay.

USB Device Access — Required to communicate with the connected Carlink USB adapter.

Local Storage (if applicable) — Used only for device-side logging or configuration storage.

Audio input is processed locally for CarPlay functionality and is not recorded, stored, or transmitted by the App.

Data Sharing

The App does not share user data with third parties.

The App does not use analytics services, advertising services, or tracking technologies.

Data Storage

All App operations occur locally on the Android Automotive system.
No user data is uploaded to remote servers.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with external cloud services.

When connected to Apple CarPlay, certain services (such as Apple services) are governed by their own privacy policies.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed toward children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected by revising the Effective Date above.
